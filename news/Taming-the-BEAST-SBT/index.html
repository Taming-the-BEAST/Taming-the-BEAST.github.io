Taming the BEAST in Squamish, Canada, 2023!

22 Feb 2023 by Tim

We are very excited to announce the next installment of Taming the BEAST, which will again be held in Squamish, BC.

The workshop will take place from August 14 to 18, 2023,

For information on the venue, prices and registration, visit the Squamish BEAST Tamers website.

Details on the programme will be posted here as soon as they are available, so check back often for updates!
