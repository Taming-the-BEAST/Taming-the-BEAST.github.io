Taming the BEAST Down Under!

06 Sep 2018 by Tim

We are delighted to announce that the next Taming the BEAST workshop will be in Sydney, Australia!

The workshop will take place from 17-22 February next year, and is being organized by the Molecular Ecology, Evolution and Phylogenetics lab at the University of Sydney.
