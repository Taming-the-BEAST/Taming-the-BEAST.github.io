Applications Open for Taming the BEAST in Beijing, 2025!

20 Mar 2025 by Louis

We are very happy to announce that applications for the next Taming the BEAST workshop, to be held at the IVPP in Bejing, China are now open!

The workshop will take place from July 14 to 18, 2025.

For further information visit the Taming the BEAST Beijing homepage or contact the organizing committee at ttb.beijing@ivpp.ac.cn.
