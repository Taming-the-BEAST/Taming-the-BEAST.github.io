Taming the BEAST returns to Switzerland!

08 Feb 2018 by Tim

We are excited to announce that the next installment of Taming the BEAST will once more be in Switzerland!

The workshop will take place from 17-22 June this year and is being organized by members of Tanja Stadler's Computational Evolution Group, cEvo. Registration is now open. For further information visit the workshop homepage or refer to the flyer below.
