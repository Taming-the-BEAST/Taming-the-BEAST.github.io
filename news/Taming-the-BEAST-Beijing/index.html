Taming the BEAST in Beijing, China, 2025!

14 Mar 2025 by Joëlle

It is our great pleasure to announce the first Taming the BEAST in China! It will take place in Beijing, July 14-18, 2025.

As in previous workshops, the goal is to offer a broad overview of everything that can be done with BEAST2, demonstrate best practices, and offer detailed advice to participants on their own dataset. The application fee is set to 1500 CNY (approx 200€), and includes all meals during the workshop. Applications will open soon on this website!

For any further questions, please contact the organizing committee at ttb.beijing@ivpp.ac.cn.
